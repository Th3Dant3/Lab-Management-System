RX BREAKAGE JOURNEY — UNDERSTANDING THE FLOW
① Job in Transit — Source to AR41
The RX job has been scanned out of the ORB or OTB source machine and is now moving through the lab toward the AR41 inspection station. This segment measures total travel and queue time — from the moment the source machine releases the job to the moment the operator picks it up at AR41.
→
② Breakage Identified — AR41 Scan to Breakage Table
The operator has scanned the job at AR41 and a power or axis defect has been detected. This segment captures how long it took from that initial scan until the breakage was formally recorded at the breakage table — the window in which the failure was confirmed and logged.
→
③ Breakage Processing — Table Scan to Reorder
The breakage has been logged and the job is now being analyzed, documented, and closed out. This segment reflects how long it took the lab to complete the full breakage workflow — from table entry through final disposition — so the job can be reprocessed or sent back for reorder.
Each bar represents one RX job. Width is proportional to time in each phase. Hover any segment for exact timestamps, operator ID, and machine details.
