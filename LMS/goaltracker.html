Improving Operational Dashboards and Reporting

I want to continue improving the dashboards and reporting tools used to track daily lab operations. These dashboards help give a clearer view of production activity, WIP, breakage, scan performance, workflow movement, and operational bottlenecks.

My goal is to make the data easier to understand, more accurate, and more useful for day-to-day decision-making.
